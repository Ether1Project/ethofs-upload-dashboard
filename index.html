WHAT IS ETHOFS?

ethoFS combines the Ether-1 Masternode/Service Node Network with IPFS to deliver decentralized content on a scale not seen before. Users will eventually be able to upload content or a website via a simple UI to be decentrally hosted with ethoFS.

WHY IS DECENTRALIZED HOSTING WITH ETHOFS SUCH A BIG DEAL?

Immutability, zero censorship and attack/DDOS resistance are just a few of the benefits. This alleviates one of the major problems/costs companies have to deal with today while using a normal centralized hosting service. Several governments and large corporate entities choose to filter and/or censor the content you are able to see.

WHAT WILL IT COST TO UTILIZE THE ETHOFS SYSTEM?

Specific costs have not been decided/determined yet but when we have reached full deployment, Users will pay for the service with ETHO. The cost will be based on the size/storage space required by the content uploaded and the user will be charged a periodic fee (in ETHO) for as long as they want that content available on the network.
